Watch Styles (Basic)

If you’re new to watchmaking, the type of watch you will likely be most intuitively associated with is the ‘dress watch’. This is simply because ‘dress watches’ have been around a lot longer than anything else, and are thus the effective archetype of what watches should look like. If your dad or grandfather wore a watch, its style (especially its diameter) would likely be considered dressy by larger, brasher, more modern standards.
